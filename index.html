Our story

Chapter One

The story of us

Some love stories follow a script. Ours was written differently, full of courage, distance, heartbreak, and the kind of love that keeps finding its way back. Here's how it all began.

March 3rd and 4th, 2022

The birthday dinner where it all began

Your birthday is March 3rd, but we went out to celebrate on the 4th. Surrounded by the warmth of that dinner, I looked at you and asked you to be mine. Maybe I wanted the celebration to mean something more, something that was just ours. You said yes, and just like that, your birthday weekend became the beginning of us.

A play at Terrakulture

Culture, you, and an evening I will not forget

There is something about experiencing art with someone you love. Sitting beside you at Terrakulture, watching the stage come alive, sneaking glances at your face to see what moved you. That evening told me a lot about who you are and made me fall a little harder.

Our pottery and painting session

Making something beautiful, together

Hands in clay, paint on fingers, probably more laughter than actual art. Our pottery and painting session was one of those dates that felt completely us. Creative, a little chaotic, and full of the kind of joy that only happens when two people are truly comfortable with each other.

My first time trying local Nigerian dishes

You fed me, literally and figuratively

You watched my face carefully with every bite, equal parts excited and nervous. Introducing me to your food felt personal, like you were sharing a piece of yourself. I want you to know, it was not just the food that I loved. It was the pride in your eyes as you waited for my reaction. That stays with me.

Dinner at 788 Restaurant

A night dressed in intention

788 was the kind of dinner that deserved a slow evening. Good food, good conversation, and you looking the way you always do when you know you are dressed well and feel it. I remember thinking somewhere between the starter and the main course that I was in trouble, the very best kind.

April 2025

A chapter we had to close

Not every love story is a straight line. We went through a season apart and it hurt. But even in that distance, in that silence, something remained. You don't fall in love with someone like you and walk away unchanged. I didn't.

December 2025, Ghana

We found our way back, and you found padel

And just like that, the right ending became a new beginning. We chose each other again in December, and you came to visit me in Ghana carrying that fresh start with you. Watching you discover padel, fearless and glowing, reminded me exactly why I never stopped loving you. Some things are simply meant to be.

March 3rd, 2026, Today

Your birthday. Our story continues.

Here we are again, a year older, a love deeper, and a bond that has been tested and proven real. Today is your day, Stephanie. And I am so glad I get to celebrate you.
